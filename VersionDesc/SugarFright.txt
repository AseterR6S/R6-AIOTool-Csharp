Operation "Shadow Legacy" introduced a single operator from another Tom Clancys franchise.
Sam Fisher codenamed Zero was added into the game.
The 1.5x scope, 2.0x scope, and the 3.0X scope were been brought to the game via this expansion.
Ubisoft also reworked the map Chalet and the first ever operator rework was released this season.
The Sugar Freight event was available to play during the operation.
