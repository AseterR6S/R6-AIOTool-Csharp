Operation "Phantom Sight" is the second season of year four.
This season introduced two new operators:
Warden who's a part of the American Secret Service,
and Nokk who belongs to the Danish Jaegercorpset.
The Showdown event took place during this season.
"Phantom Sight" also presented a Kafe Dostoyevsky rework.
